Pop and Convert is a customizable pop-up and notification WordPress plugin to boost engagement, guide visitors, and generate leads.

== Description ==

If you want to boost your website's engagement and drive conversion, the Pop and Convert plugin is here to help.

Pop and Convert is a pop-up and notification WordPress plugin for bloggers, marketers, and business owners. This plugin enhances website engagement through customizable notifications, helping you guide visitors and generate leads.

You can customize colors and fonts and choose from various layouts to ensure your notifications resonate perfectly with your brand's identity. Additionally, you can choose from sticky pop-ups to center-stage pop-ups and decide how and where your notifications appear on your website.

With this WordPress plugin, you can inform your website visitors, build loyalty, and ensure every page gets noticed.

[Documentation](https://popandconvert.com/docs/) | [Purchase Pro](https://popandconvert.com/pricing/) | [Support](https://popandconvert.com/support-ticket/)

With Pop and Convert, you can create pop-ups such as:

* **Sticky Footer Pop-ups:** Notifications that remain anchored at the bottom of the page, ensuring visibility without obstructing content.
* **Centered Pop-ups:** Attention-grabbing alerts in the middle of the screen, ideal for important announcements or offers.
* **Page Load Pop-ups:** Automatically appear when a visitor lands on a specific page or post.
* **Timed Pop-ups:** Appear after a user has spent some time on the site.
* **Device-Specific Pop-ups:** Tailored notifications for desktop, tablet, or mobile users.

### Features of Pro Version
* **Content Settings:** You can select from different layout options for your pop-ups. We have two layout options for sticky notifications and three for overlay notifications.
* **Single Post/Page Settings:** You can choose when the notification appears on your website. There are two options to choose from:
    1. **On-Page Load:** Notification appears after the web page loads fully.
    2. **On-Page Exit Intent:** Notification shows when a visitor is about to exit your site.
* **Create Notification Via Page/Post Editor:** After activating the license key, you can create notifications directly in the Page/Post editor. Notifications made via the Page editor have higher priority than those from the dashboard. They will appear on your site's front end.
